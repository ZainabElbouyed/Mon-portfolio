About Me

Let me introduce myself

I'm a 20-year-old Moroccan engineering student at INPT, Rabat, specializing in Ubiquitous and Distributed Systems - Cloud & IoT.

Passionate about web development, artificial intelligence and technological innovation, I love building smart, efficient, and scalable digital experiences.

I thrive in environments where creativity meets logic. My skills range from front-end development and API design to machine learning and embedded systems. I'm always looking for ways to bring ideas to life through code.

Whether it’s designing intuitive web interfaces, exploring AI for smarter systems, or imagining the next breakthrough, I’m driven by curiosity, purpose, and a passion for shaping the future through technology.

My Core Values

How I Learn and Grow

I believe in the power of curiosity, resilience, and collaboration. These values guide my journey as a student and aspiring engineer.

I’m also driven by a strong sense of purpose: technology should serve people. That’s why I aim to build solutions that are useful, inclusive, and sustainable.
